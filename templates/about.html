Brief History

Pioneering Excellence Since 2024 In the dynamic landscape of recruitment and career advancement, FutuRelevance Connect Connect emerged as a trailblazer in 2024, founded by the visionary Managing Director, Greg Barnardo. With a passion for connecting talent with opportunity and a commitment to redefining the recruitment experience, the company swiftly carved its niche in the industry.

Founding Vision

Established with a vision to bridge the gap between exceptional talents and forward-thinking organizations, FutuRelevance Connect set out to revolutionize the jobs portal and recruitment sector. Greg Barnardo, a seasoned professional with a wealth of experience as a public servant in government, brought a unique perspective to the helm. His insight into government operations and understanding of public service dynamics infused the company with a commitment to integrity, transparency, and inclusivity.

Government Expertise

Greg Barnardo's background as a public servant in government significantly shaped FutuRelevance Connect approach to recruitment. Drawing on his extensive experience, the company developed a nuanced understanding of the skills, competencies, and values crucial in public service roles. This expertise proved instrumental in establishing FutuRelevance Connect as a preferred partner for government and corporate institutions seeking top-tier talent.

From its inception, FutuRelevance Connect embraced innovation, leveraging cutting-edge technology and data-driven insights to transform the recruitment process. The company's commitment to excellence, coupled with Greg Barnardo's public service acumen, fuelled its success in delivering tailored solutions that meet the unique demands of both candidates and government agencies.
